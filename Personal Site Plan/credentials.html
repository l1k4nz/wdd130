Credentials of Juan Condori - Instructor

Juan Condori has been a Instructor teaching since 2007 and has been a professor enthusiast for his entire life. Because of his math knowledge, tecnology, and compassion his training services have been requested all over Tacna-Perú.

He recertifies every two years. He attends multiple training seminars every year to keep his knowledge current and his skills up to date. This training is recognized worldwide as the best possible. He also spends several days each year in the same use of training that is received and required of law enforcement officers so that he can share with you information that you will not learn otherwise.
