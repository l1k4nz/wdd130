Our Philosophy

Everyone should have basic firearm safety training. Our goal is to provide a safe, no pressure training environment that allows each person to learn at their own comfort level. We want everyone to attain a proficient level of gun safety and firearms knowledge.

Education

More than 41% of US households have at least one firerarm. You, and your family, need to know how to be safe around firearms. We teach you firearm safety, even if you don't own one. Watching movies or Internet videos will not replace live training with qualified instructors.

Self Defense

Self defense is not just about defending yourself. You need to understand the mindset and responsibilities of carrying or deploying a firearm. Each state has different laws and you need to know them before you find yourself at risk. You need to understand what to expect, and what to do, if you are forced into a self defense situation.
