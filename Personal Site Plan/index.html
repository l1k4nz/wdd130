Intro

Imagine combining: Learning by doing, playful imagination, analytical rigor, curiosity, passion and service. Our teachers, highly demanding according to international standards of educational excellence, are committed to teaching with practice. They set the example by working in the university's own facilities in areas related to their teaching subject.

Our Mission

Make the best free private university in the world and make education accessible to all, regardless of race, gender, belief, socioeconomic position.We believe that the most powerful weapon of a nation is its education. And we believe that ignorance and misinformation is the root of all evil

Teamwork:

Understand people to understand the world. We believe that individually you can achieve many things, but in a group you can go even further. We believe that when the spirit of teamwork wins, in reality we all win. Therefore we believe in open information. Inclusive:In our university discrimination is prohibited, everyone is encouraged not to use labels, prejudices of any kind to anyone or oneself. We support the different multiple intelligences and adapt our curriculum and specialized teachers for each group of students with different types of learning, recognizing the country's biodiversity and multiculturalism. Creative:On our campus there are no bad ideas, they are all potentially powerful ideas capable of improving the world, and with the proper direction and patience, they will be fulfilled
