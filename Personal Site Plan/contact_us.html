When I know what classes I want to take, what do I do?


When you are ready to register for a class, visit our Class Schedules page and click on the "Contact Us" link. Send us an email with your name and the names of anyone who would like to attend the class. We will e-mail you confirming your attendance, the payment due at the class, supply details needed for class, and provide directions to the range.

If you reserve a spot and do not notify us 24 hours prior to the class that you will not be attending, you will be placed on our PAY IN ADVANCE list for future classes.

For more information, please contact us using the form below, or call (949)697-2694.
