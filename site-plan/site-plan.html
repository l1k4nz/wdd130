Overview

Purpose

We are here to provide you with an unforgettable adventure! You will have thrills, maybe some spills, and possibly some chills as you go with us to explore a river and see scenery that you won't see from the back seat of a car.

Audience

We offer this adventure to families, friends, groups, and individuals. The only prerequisite is that you must be ready to get wet and can safely wear and use a life preserver.

Typography

Heading Font: Permanent Marker

I selected this font because it evokes images of boxes of pictures and keepsakes that I want to tie to the theme of a treasured adventure. It is a comfortable font that everyone has used and it is not intimidating.

Paragraph Font: Nunito

Nunito has a casual feel to it but it also has a rigid structure. So it looks official without being threatening.

Normal paragraph example
