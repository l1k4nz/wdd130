Purpose

White water rafting company website is all about offering quality online booking for tourist, advanced, effective services and solutions to people online and even at the comfort of their homes. Also, to attract prospective tourist who would love to experience trips tailored to all levels of experience and Exhilarating whitewater tours of colorado magnificent rivers and spectacular wilderness setting. Expertise, programs, and services on the rivers. We create services to trust and improve tourist-tour guides engagements. We also provide support information for families. This is basically to convert potential tourist into appointments and/or requests for second opinions. The efficacy of service, and overall outcome is imperative as far as the provision of white water river services are concerned at White Water rafting company. Client's do not have to wait in a queue to have access to quality services.

Audience

White water rafting company would be ideal for individuals and families that are middle income earners,High income earners, and the rich or wealthy people within the age group of seventeen to sixty six years old and love to use the internet or technology to simplify their lives. These are group of people who live a luxury lifestyle or middle level luxury to some point and very particular about their time and mental health. They envision or imagine a life of access to quality travel and pleasurable services. These group of individuals do not want to join queues or being on a wait list to have the pleasure of having access to white water tours and enjoying the rivers. These group of demographic or potential clients are very sensitive or emotional about how their leisure time and needs can be met professionally, even so it's imperative that they get the needed attention without hassle or stress. They will be users of Mobile phones, Laptops, desktops, and tablets that have access to the internet.

The Site Map of a site is just like it sounds…it is a map of the pages in a site and how they are related and linked together. From the map above we can see that we will eventually have the Home page and 2 sub or child pages.

The lines that connect them all together indicate that each page should be accessible from any other page, it is essentially showing us the global navigation for the site.

Wireframes

Wireframes are like blueprints for making webpages. They should show the major sections of content that will be on the page and the relative locations of each element. In the wireframe below you can see there will be 6 sections to our page:

  1. At the top we have a section with the logo (the box with the mountain means an image) and the navigation bar.
  2. Then there is a banner image that stretches all the way across the screen.
  3. Next we have some text and an image
  4. ...followed by another row made up of an image and some text.
  5. Then one more section of text with no image.
  6. Lastly, a footer containing a copyright/name line and 3 social media icons.
