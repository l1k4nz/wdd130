Canete River
Lima

Just 180 km southeast of the city of Lima, this river allows canoeing all year round. However, between November and April is when the flow is greatest. In its most visited section, in the Lunahuan√° valley, you will find class II, III and IV rapids.

Mayo River
Tarapoto

This river is one of the most recommended for those who want to start rafting. Its rapids are level II and III and it has many sections where it is safe to let yourself be carried away by the current until you reach the shore. Rafting on the Mayo River is suitable for children from 5 years old.

Vilcanota river
Sacred Valley of Cusco

this river allows rafting of all levels of difficulty. The sections for beginners are located between the town of Calca and Urubamba, and in the district of Pisac. During the rainy season, between December and April, the increase in the water level makes it difficult to practice canoeing.
