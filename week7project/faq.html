Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Is this river dangerous?

A. Any body of water should be treated as potentially dangerous. Our guides are experienced and they know the river and it's changing conditions to make it the safest experience possible.

Q. What equipment do I need to bring?

A. We provide all rafting equipment and life vests. We recommend that you bring insect repllant, sports or water shoes, sun screen, hat. Be prepared to get wet!

Q. What happens if it rains the day of our trip?

A. We ride rain or shine. This is a wet adventure. The only exception is if we experience lightning.

Q. Can we bring our own group?

A. Yes. Sort of. Our boats can hold up to 10 people so we may ask you to move around to balance the boats.
